Preheat oven to 350°F. In a large bowl mix the graham cracker crumbs, butter, granulated sugar, and salt together evenly. Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of 9-inch springform pan. Bake the crust for 8-10 minutes, or until lightly golden. Set aside while you make the cheesecake.
